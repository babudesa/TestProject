Using Flags
Only a supervisor or manager of the group to which the claim is assigned (or the supervisor of any parent group) can remove a flag from a claim. Typically, a supervisor attends to the issue, and after completing the task, removes the flag.

Navigate to the Team tab and drill down to a specific user.

The reason shows under the Latest Notes section on the Summary screen of the claim.
