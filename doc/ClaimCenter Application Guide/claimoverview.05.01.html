ClaimCenter Application Guide : Introduction : Claims Overview
Claims Overview
To insurance carriers, a claim is a collection of all the information related to an accident or loss of some kind. A ClaimCenter claim is analogous to a physical claim file that collects and records in one place all the information relating to the claim. Unlike a physical file, a ClaimCenter claim also records and tracks the progress of all work involved in handling the claim.
This topic briefly introduces you to the features of ClaimCenter.
