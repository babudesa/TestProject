Differences Between Notes and Documents
Notes and documents have distinct functions in ClaimCenter, and the application handles them differently. The following table highlights the main differences. See “Document Management”.
 
Characteristics of Notes
Characteristics of Documents
Written in plain text.
Can have many different MIME types, such as PDF, Word, or Excel.
Created by a user or Gosu.
Created by a user or Gosu, or come from an external document management system.
Stored only in the database.
Stored either in the database or a document management system.
Related to one claim or claim entity.
Can be related to one claim, linked to many others, and attached to notes.
