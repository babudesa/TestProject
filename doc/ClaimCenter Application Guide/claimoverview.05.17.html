Litigation
For claims that involve legal action, the Legal Matters screen shows the legal matters that are pertinent to the claim. A matter is the set of data organized around a single lawsuit or potential lawsuit. A matter includes information on the attorneys involved, the trial details, and the lawsuit details.
See “Legal Matters”.
