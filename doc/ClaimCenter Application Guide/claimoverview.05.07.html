Incidents in ClaimCenter
ClaimCenter tracks incidents, such as issues or accidents, that can result in claims. Some examples include:
•
One of your insured clients is in an automobile accident and has filed a claim with your insurance company.
•
Someone slips and falls at a store owned by one of your insured clients, but has not yet filed a claim. The store owner contacts your insurance company to ensure that the incident is recorded with you.
Incidents are explained in the section called “Incidents”.
