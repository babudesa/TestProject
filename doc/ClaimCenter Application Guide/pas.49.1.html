Policy System Integration
The default configuration of ClaimCenter includes a functional integration with Guidewire PolicyCenter. You must integrate PolicyCenter with ClaimCenter yourself. You can also integrate ClaimCenter with the policy system of your choice. This topic describes how ClaimCenter integrates with a policy system in general and PolicyCenter in particular.
