Assigning Work
All work in ClaimCenter has an owner - someone who is responsible for making sure the work is done properly. After work is assigned to someone, that ClaimCenter user becomes its owner. All work is assigned to both a user and group. See “What Work is Assignable” for a definition of work.
Work is often assigned when it is first created, but can be assigned or reassigned later to a different owner. A powerful feature of ClaimCenter is that it can make assignments automatically, based on rules which model your business practices. ClaimCenter also provides the ability to assign work manually, that is managers and supervisors can freely select to whom they assign work.
