Email Enhancements
ClaimCenter provides enhanced ability to send emails from within ClaimCenter. You may generate emails using GScript, GScript classes, and rules. GScript supports:
•
Sending to different types of recipients: To:, CC:, and BCC: recipients.
•
Attaching documents stored in the configured document management system.
•
Using templates to populate the Subject and Body of the email.
•
Defining activity patterns with an associated email template.
•
Creating Activities with a Send Email button that use a particular email template.
For more information, see “Outbound Email” in the Application Guide.
There is also a user interface that allows users to create emails in the course of their work.
