Coverage Model Changes
The ClaimCenter coverage model, or how it defines Lines of Business, has been reworked. The six existing typelists are more complete and flexible. See “Configuring Your Lines of Business” in the Application Guide for a complete description. In addition, a new LOB editor, described in the Studio Guide, allow you to configure new LOBs in a simple way.
